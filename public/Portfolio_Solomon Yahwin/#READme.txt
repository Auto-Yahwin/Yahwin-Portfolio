**********************SOLIDWORKS PORTFOLIO [!st Eleven Folders]**************************

The first 11 folders contain selected SolidWorks models and technical drawings that
represent my recent CAD work.
The files demonstrate skills in 2D sketching, 3D modeling, assembly creation, and
part detailing.

Files Included:
- 2D sketches: Contains some basic solidworks sketches aimed to demonstrate
	       my sketch competence.
- Aeroplane: The project features a narow bodied, low wing T-tail aircaft.
	     I implemented material assignment and physical appearance on the model.
- Bottle cover: This is a simple bottle cover with saw-tooth edges
- Bridge bracket: A simple structural part component to demonstrate 3D modelling
- Cam-follower assembly: An assembly drawing to showcase part drawing and assembling
- Connecting rod assembly: This project features an assembly. Material assignment, part
			   detailing (exploded view) and reporting (use of balloons and
			   Bill of materials) were also incorporated.
- Flow junction: A part drawing of flow channel intended for ANSYS analysis
- Four-seater aircraft: A CAD model of a four seater low wing concept aircraft
- Karet tail UAV: A CAD model of an unconventional tail UAV
- Piston-connecting rod assembly: A sequel of the connecting rod project. crankshaft, and
				  piston drawn then assembled
- T bracket: A common solidwork part model to demonstrate simple 3D modelling techniques

All drawings are saved in their native SolidWorks format, along with exported PDFs and
JPEGs for easier viewing.

These files showcase my current proficiency in SolidWorks and my attention to design intent, constraints, and detailing.
